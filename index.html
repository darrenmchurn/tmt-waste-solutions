What Can Go In?

✓ Accepted

  • Household junk & furniture
  • Construction debris (wood, drywall, concrete)
  • Roofing shingles
  • Yard waste & brush
  • Appliances (no freon)
  • Flooring & carpet
  • General renovation debris

✗ Not Accepted

  • Hazardous waste
  • Tires
  • Batteries
  • Paint & solvents
  • Asbestos
  • Medical / biohazard waste
  • Freon-containing appliances
